6pm

It's 6pm and i'm lost
Lost in the Memories
Of all the things
that came at a cost

It's 6pm and i'm scared
Scared of these thoughts
Thoughts that are inside
my mind

It's 6pm and the sky's on fire

its 6:01 pm yet I'm still
burning for desire


Zariyah
