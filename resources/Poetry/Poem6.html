The Beautiful Poem

What she saw was amazing
What she felt was love
What she was meant to be
Was a poem of beauty

She wanted to be her owen
Knight in shining armour,
Her own King
Her own everything

BUT

Society had different plans
for Her...
And Like most who came before
She fell victim to Society

So now you see
its really sucks
that she could'nt be
what she always wanted to be

she was never ugly
Society was the ugly one

So now all she sees
is a feak
All she feels
is Hate

R.I.P.
To the Beautiful Poem
That will never be...


Zariyah
