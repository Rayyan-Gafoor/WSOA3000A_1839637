Twenty6

Twenty six hundered songs, poems and letters gone wrong.
Things i wrote all in your name, I guess you'll never know.
You'll never know this pain.

Twenty 6 thousand lies, things I've said, never said why...
I chose to keep it all deep inside, kept all those thought I had,
Every single One still trapped in my mind.

Twenty six hundered days, As well as nights, I see your face.
But those Three Words I never say, every chance i got,
I threw them all away.

Twenty 6 thousand hours, the time I've spent locked in my tower
Trpped in this self-inflicted loneliness, thinking about you
I'm left starring at the stars, and just another lonely Moon.


Zariyah
