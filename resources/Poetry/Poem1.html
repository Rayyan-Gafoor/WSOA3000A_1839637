Paper Hearts

A thousand paper clips
holding two paper hearts
Oragami Love,
holographically wrriten in the stars

A secret language,
none has ever really understood
But I try to learn it anyway
so this is what i'll do...

i'll send those paper planes
to your heart shaped box
for betweeen those lines of grey
are the words i fail to say


Zariyah
