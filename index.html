About Me!

Hi there, My name is Zariyah. Welcome to my website. I am a historian as well as war photographer. I've recently decided to start writing poetry. I've been classified by society as an introvert, and honestly they are right. I dont really believe in religion, but i do believe in a higher power.
Im still kinda trying to figure out where I fit into this universe as well as my purpose. And well I guess for now I hope enjoy my content.

My First War

They say you always remember your first, well they are right. On my first day at war, I was joined by three other journalist. I was excited, to be honest it felt like I was going on an adventure. It all felt like a movie, like I was reliving my favourite game. In the begining everything felt calm, but then everything suddenly changed almost as fast as I could say: "Shit".
