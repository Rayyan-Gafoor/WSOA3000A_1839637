Im still kinda trying to figure out where I fit into this universe as well as my purpose. And well I guess for now I hope enjoy my content.

My First War

They say you always remember your first, well they are right. On my first day at war, I was joined by three other journalist. I was excited, to be honest it felt like I was going on an adventure. It all felt like a movie, like I was reliving my favourite game. In the begining everything felt calm, but then everything suddenly changed almost as fast as I could say: "Shit". WAR PHOTOGRAPHY

So recently I have discorvered that I have a love for space, well not really recently, more like somethhing that always been there, but gradually died out. Well now its back. ALSO FunFact:
When I was little and thought about being in this hell hole we call adulthood, I always wanted to be an Inter-Galactic Space-Soldier. To be honest i loved the idea of travelling and exploring space, and when I heard my father was a soldier, I said hey, I could be like my dad and protect our universe. Thus:

Inter-Galactic Space-Soldier.

Anyway I would always find myself staring into the vast unknown of space, looking at NASA's latest pictures and honestly sometimes I'd talk to the stars. Well actually one star in particular,

Antares

, The heart of the Scorpian. She has always been like a personal therapist for me (is that weird, nah, I don't think its weird, is it?)

Anyway if you are into these kinds of things like me, take a look at NASA's Picture of the Day.
